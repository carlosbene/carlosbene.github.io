SPACE Game Studio

SPACE wants to become the first aerospace-focused game studio in Toulouse (France), in partnership with the local aerospace companies, institutions and associations. Our goal is to create simple and innovative videogames experiences, bringing art and science together. Our first project in progress is Mothership, a peaceful exploration game mixing adventure and real astronomy. In parallel, we are developing the StarMap, an educational version of this game’s space systems for museums and educational institutions.

Its founder is Carlos BENEDITO, a former aeronautical engineer and independent game designer. He was born in the mediterranean city of Valencia, Spain. He got his degree in the Polytechnic University of Madrid where he participated in the UPM/Sat-2 microsatellite project (successfully launched in 2020). Afterwards he moved to Toulouse where he has been working in Design Office for several Airbus projects. He began his entrepreneur venture from scratch in the spring of 2018. The 3 main activities so far have been prototyping with Unreal Engine 4, gathering a local team and involving the local aerospace entities and science communicators.


MOTHERSHIP

Mothership will be our first videogame. It is a dreamy sci-fi exploration adventure game. You will travel across the real star systems of our own galaxy looking for other space refugees like youself, to live in peace. During your search you will track and recover the Black-Boxes of huge crashed spaceships (the Motherships) in order to recover its fuel and be able to continue searching deeper into space. It is about pathfinding in handcrafted awe-inspiring worlds with some hidden environmental dangers and rush moments.

Adventure:
  • Pathfinding - Find your own way to the Motherships' Black-Boxes in exotic open worlds
  • Scout gear - Use your scout gear to access new ways: cut any object with your nano-machete, rappel with your rope from anywhere ...
  • Environment dangers - Spot and overcome the hidden environmental traps
  • Motherships’ wrecks - Explore the spooky Motherships’ wrecks to recover its Black-Box
  • Rush return - Return with the Black-Box's fuel to your ship while escaping from catastrophic events
Relax:
  • Feel at home - Take a break and chill out in your ship’s dreamy atmosphere
  • Playground - Enjoy listening the aerial soundtrack, playing mini-games or just going outside for a space walk
  • The Cupola - Contemplate the surface of the planets from orbit in your ship’s cupola
Science:
  • Entering Zero-G - Feel the zero gravity like never before: drift like a real astronaut or fly like a rocket-man
  • Exoplanets - Experience different gravities and light/night cycles on each exoplanet
  • Orbital search - Control your ship orbit trajectory in real time to perform reentry and rendezvous maneuvers
  • Star path - Travel to the most famous stars of the night sky: Alpha Centauri, Sirius, Vega ...
  • Augmented reallity - Understand the planets’ mouvement thanks to your augmented-reality vision
  • Theory of relativity - A life-span adventure due to Einstein’s time dilation when travelling close to the speed of light

STARMAP

The StarMap is an improved version of the space systems that you will use in the Mothership game. It is an educative software that can be used to visualize and interact with the International Space Station, the Solar System and well ... the whole observable universe ! This system could be the best one ever created to understand the most basic astronomy and astrophysic phenomena because it is the simplest to use. Hence, it is an excellent support for museums, educational institutions and invested parents.

  • Universe - Zoom in and out from the solar system to the edge of the observable universe
  • Solar System - Visit every planet and moon and feel the different movement of the sky from each one of them
  • Low Earth Orbit - Get into orbit and pilot the International Space Station
